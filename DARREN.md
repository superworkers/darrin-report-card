# Darrin's Framework: How the Six Questions Map to the Four Challenges

## The Four Challenges

The report card reports on four challenges:

1. Generating cold and qualified leads
2. Conducting a discovery call
3. Losing a prospect after the discovery call
4. Closing sales

## How Each Question Informs the Challenges

**Question 1 — How are you different from your competitors?**
If they have a low score there, that will hurt in generating qualified leads, and it will spill into conducting poor discovery calls with unqualified prospects.

**Question 2 — Describe your primary target audience in one sentence.**
If that gives you a poor grade, then you will have a very difficult time generating cold and qualified leads. And that also will spill into a poor discovery call and sales process because they won't be qualified.

**Question 3 — List the pain points your primary target audience faces.**
If you get a low score there, that will hurt your discovery call. That will hurt your losing prospects after the discovery call, and it will absolutely affect your closing ratio negatively.

**Question 5 — Describe your lead magnet.**
If that has a low score, then you will suffer with generating cold and qualified leads.

**Question 6 — Describe the steps of your sales process or funnel.**
If you have a low score there, that will hurt you in closing sales primarily.
